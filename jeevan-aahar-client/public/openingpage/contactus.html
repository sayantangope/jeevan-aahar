Jeevan Ahaar is a platform that connects food donors with people in need, ensuring surplus food is shared instead of wasted. Our motto: "A Meal. A Smile. A Life."
‚ùì How can I donate food?
You can register as a donor on our website, select your donor type (individual/organization), and submit details about your food donation. Our team or volunteers will guide you through the next steps!
‚ùì Who receives the food donations?
We distribute food to verified NGOs, shelters, and directly to communities that need it the most, ensuring safe and timely delivery.
‚ùì Is it safe to donate cooked food?
Yes, but we request that all donated cooked food be freshly prepared, hygienically packed, and shared within safe time limits to maintain quality.
‚ùì Do I need to pay anything to donate?
No. Jeevan Ahaar is a completely free platform for both donors and receivers. We believe kindness should have no cost.
